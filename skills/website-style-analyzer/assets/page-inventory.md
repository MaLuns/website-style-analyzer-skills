## Status Definitions

- `todo`: queued, not analyzed
- `in_progress`: currently being analyzed
- `done`: analysis complete and recorded
- `skipped`: intentionally skipped (reason required in notes)

## URL Canonicalization Checklist

- Remove tracking params such as `utm_*`
- Remove hash fragments
- Normalize trailing slash rules
- Normalize protocol and domain casing
